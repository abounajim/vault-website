Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my bank statement?

Tap the + button on the Transactions tab, then select "Import Statement." You can upload a PDF bank statement or take a photo. Our AI will extract your transactions automatically.

What's included in VAULT Pro?

VAULT Pro includes 1 AI insight per day, 15 AI chat messages per day, Daily Roast, 30 OCR pages per month, and full monthly financial reports. A 7-day free trial is available.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store on your iPhone → tap your profile → Subscriptions → find VAULT → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of the billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open VAULT → tap the upgrade button → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored in Google Firebase's encrypted cloud infrastructure with strict security rules. Only you can access your financial data. We never sell your data to third parties.

Which languages does VAULT support?

VAULT supports English, Arabic, French, Spanish, Portuguese, and Chinese. You can change the language in Settings → Language.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

The OCR import didn't detect all my transactions. What do I do?

You can manually add missed transactions using the + button. OCR accuracy depends on the quality of your bank statement. For best results, use a clear PDF export from your bank's app rather than a photo.
