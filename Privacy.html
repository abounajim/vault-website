Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

VAULT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VAULT mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you register
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, account balances, and budget information you enter or import
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • OCR Data: Bank statement images and PDFs you upload for transaction extraction (processed and not stored permanently)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the VAULT service
  • To generate AI-powered financial insights personalized to you
  • To process bank statement imports via OCR technology
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To improve and optimize the app experience
  • To send important service notifications
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat

Third-Party Services

VAULT uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions — Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: OCR and document processing — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude: AI chat and insights generation — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and billing — Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics (Firebase): Anonymous usage analytics

Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored securely in Google Firebase's encrypted cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account access
  • Biometric authentication support (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Server-side security rules restricting data access to account owners only

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Service providers necessary to operate the app (listed above)
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

VAULT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Export your financial data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of VAULT after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
